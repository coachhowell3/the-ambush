From the Head Coach
Summer Camp is done. The work speaks for itself.

Tiger families — Summer Camp is in the books, and I couldn't be more proud of what this group did. Twenty-nine players showed up every day. Ten did not miss a single practice. Two players — Zaiden Baker and Benjamin Bierner — made all 35 voluntary workouts from May through July. Every. Single. One. That kind of commitment is rare, and it doesn't go unnoticed.

We finished camp the right way — with an all-night lock-in at the high school. These guys are building something together, and you can feel it.

Now we turn the page. The schedule is out. Fall Camp starts August 3. The Ambush is coming — and it starts in two weeks.

IMPORTANT: Every player must have a physical for the 2026–2027 school year before they can practice. High school practice starts August 3. Middle school practice starts August 10. Get your physical done now.

— Coach Howell  |  Hot Springs Tigers Football

All 35 Voluntary Workouts — May through July
Perfect Summer — Zaiden Baker & Benjamin Bierner

Out of every player in the program, two made all 35 voluntary workouts from the first day of the Foundation Teaching Block in May through the last day of Summer Camp in July. Zaiden Baker (Class of 2028) and Benjamin Bierner (Class of 2030). Not a single absence. That's what elite preparation looks like. The rest of the program knows who to follow.

Squad of the Summer
Week after week, all summer long, Andrew Torres's squad led the program in attendance and put the most players on the perfect attendance list. Every single week. That's not luck — that's leadership.
