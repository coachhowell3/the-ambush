From the Head Coach
Summer Camp starts now. This is where teams are built.

Tiger families — happy Fourth of July. We hope you're enjoying the holiday with your families. The Tigers spent part of their Fourth giving back to the community, and you'll see that story below.

The last two weeks have been about more than just speed and strength. We introduced The Blueprint — our mental performance curriculum — because we believe great football players are built from the inside out. What's in your mind shows up on the field. We've been working on that.

We also took the defense to Silver City and liked what we saw. One score allowed in the game portions against Silver and Cobre. The offense has work to do — and that's exactly what Summer Camp is for. Camp starts Monday, July 6. This is where teams are made. We want every Tiger in that building.

— Coach Howell  |  Hot Springs Tigers Football

Community
Tigers Help Light Up the Sky

On the Fourth of July, a group of Tigers and coaches spent their holiday helping set up the fireworks display at Elephant Butte Lake. Coaches, players, desert landscape, and a whole lot of mortar tubes — that's how Hot Springs Tigers celebrate Independence Day.

This is what it looks like when a program is woven into the fabric of its community. These guys didn't have to be there. They chose to be. That's the culture.

Five Weeks Running
Andrew Torres's Squad — Squad of the Week

Five straight weeks. Every single week this summer, Andrew Torres's squad has led the program in attendance and put the most players on the perfect attendance list. That's not a coincidence — that's leadership. Whatever standard Torres is setting, it's contagious. That's exactly what this program is built on.

Mental Performance
The Blueprint
We don't just train bodies. We train minds. The Blueprint is our mental performance curriculum — built to develop the mental habits that separate good players from great ones.
Week 1 of The Blueprint
OADB — On a Daily Basis
What are you doing on a daily basis to get better? Not once a week. Not when you feel like it. Every single day. OADB is the foundation — the idea that championships aren't won on game day, they're won in the daily habits you build long before anyone is watching.
Week 2 of The Blueprint
Are You Serious? — Rest & Recovery
Sleep. Nutrition. Hydration. These aren't soft topics — they're performance variables. A player who doesn't sleep enough is slower, weaker, and more likely to get hurt. We don't train hard just to give it back by not recovering. If you're serious about being great, recovery is part of the work.
7-on-7 Recap
Defense Shines at Silver City

The Tigers traveled to Silver City to compete in a 7-on-7 against Silver and Cobre. The defense was the story of the night — physical, disciplined, and locked in from the start.

The offense still has work to do, and the coaching staff knows it. That's what Summer Camp is for. The foundation is there. The details will come.
