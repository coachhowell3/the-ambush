From the Head Coach
The Climb is underway. Now it's time to keep climbing.

Tiger families — welcome back to The Ambush. We've got a lot to cover from the last two weeks, and even more to look forward to.

The final week of school brought some of our best energy and attendance of the entire pre-season. These guys showed up, competed, and got better every day. Now we've transitioned into summer workouts — The Climb — and the opportunity in front of this team is as big as the effort they're willing to put in.

Summer workouts are Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday mornings. Report time is 7:45 AM. We're on the field and moving by 8:00 AM and usually wrapped up by 9:30 AM. That's less than two hours. If you want to be great in August, the work is done in June and July. We want to see every Tiger in that building.

The best is still ahead.

— Coach Howell  |  Hot Springs Tigers Football

Tigers Night Out · Sidekixx · June 2, 2026
Team Building
Coach Terrazas Takes the Team Out

On Tuesday night, June 2, Coach Terrazas treated the team to a night out — bowling, pizza, and sodas at Sidekixx. It was a chance to step away from the weight room and just be a team together. Coaches and players, side by side, competing for fun.

This is what program culture looks like off the field. Big thanks to Coach Terrazas for making it happen and to Sidekixx for hosting the Hot Springs Tigers.
