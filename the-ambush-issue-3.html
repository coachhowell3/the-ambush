From the Head Coach
We went to Socorro. We competed. Now we keep climbing.

Tiger families — a lot happened over the last two weeks and there's even more coming up. I want to start by saying how proud I am of the 24 players who made the trip to Socorro last night for the 7-on-7 and Lineman Challenge. That's what this program is about — showing up, competing, and representing Truth or Consequences with class.

We saw some really good things out there. We also saw some things we need to work on. That's exactly what these competitions are for. The evaluation never stops, and neither does the work.

Summer Camp starts July 6. The schedule is loaded, the opportunities are real, and the players who put in the work right now will be the ones playing in August. Keep climbing.

— Coach Howell  |  Hot Springs Tigers Football
7-on-7 & Lineman Challenge · Socorro · June 18, 2026
On the Road
Tigers Travel to Socorro

Twenty-four Tigers made the trip to Socorro on June 18 for a 7-on-7 and Lineman Challenge. This was the program's first road competition of the summer — a chance to measure up against outside competition and see where we stand.

The coaching staff came away encouraged by what they saw and clear-eyed about what needs work. That combination is exactly where you want to be in June. The answers will come through the reps between now and August.

Three Weeks Running
Andrew Torres's Squad — Squad of the Week
Three consecutive weeks. Andrew Torres's squad has claimed Squad of the Week honors three times in a row. Leadership, accountability, competition — whatever he's doing, it's working. That's what this program is built on.

⚠️ Attention — Class of 2027 Senior Families

If your son is a member of the Class of 2027 and plans to play football this fall, he must be signed up no later than Friday, July 3, 2026.

After this date, no new seniors will be added to the roster unless they are incoming transfer students. This policy ensures our coaching staff can properly plan installs, depth charts, and equipment for the season.

If you've already filled out the form AND have been personally contacted by Coach Howell, you do not need to fill it out again. If you're not sure or haven't yet met individually with Coach Howell, please go ahead and fill it out to be safe.

Questions? Reach out to Coach Howell directly.
